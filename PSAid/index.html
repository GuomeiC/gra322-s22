Program Book/ PASid

Project Brief:

Led by the Bureau for Humanitarian Assistance (BHA), the PSAid contest was developed to challenge college students to produce public service announcements (PSAs) that creatively articulate why “Cash is Best” when helping people affected by emergencies overseas. The recent crisis in Haiti reinforces the importance of cash donations to reputable relief organizations working on the ground so they can purchase exactly what is most urgently needed to help survivors.
