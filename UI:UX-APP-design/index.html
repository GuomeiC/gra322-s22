Project Brief:

The concept of my app is helping people find a place to enjoy their day off. The theme of the app is more nature and fun. In the beginning of the app, I used XD to make some auto animation as the intro. On my home page, I used cards to organize the place categories. On the menu bar, people can research their location, check the places they saved and manage their portfolio. The last one is the one webpage responsive design.
