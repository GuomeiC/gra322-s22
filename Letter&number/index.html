Program Book/ Letter&number

Project Brief:

This is primarily a form project. To begin, there is no criteria imposed on the symbol design other than combining to create good form. Your task is to create/design an innovative symbol combining existing letter/letter or letter/number that function as one. Once the symbol is designed, a fictitious company name will be selected that connects the norms of that industry with the look and feel of the symbol.

I draw the letter Q, the letter Z, and the number 8. I used Letter Z and number 8 to make a drink shop logo.
